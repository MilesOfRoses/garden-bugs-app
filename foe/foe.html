"Foe" Bugs

Identify your pest insect

Anything that eats your crop is a "pest". There are two general types of pest insects.


One type are flying pests that can land directly on your plant.


The other type are crawling pests that must climb up your plant.


Flying pests must be delt with in different ways than crawling ones.


The easiest way to tell the difference between these types is to look for any wings.


Does your pest bug have wings?
