Garden Bugs

Friend or Foe?

Fewer than 10% of insects are actually harmful to gardens. Beneficial insects will either pollinate a plant, or they will prey on the unwanted insects.

Look up information on “Friend” bugs as well as “Foe” bugs, and find out how to organically get rid of your Foes.
