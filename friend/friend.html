"Friend" Bugs

Identify your beneficial insect

There are two types of beneficial insects.
One type are predators that feed on pests.


The other type are polinators that assist the reproduction cycle of your plants.


The easiest way to tell the difference between these types is to watch them for a while to see what they like to eat.


What is your bug eating?
  • Bug eats other bugs
  • Bug drinks nectar
  • Bug eats my crops!
